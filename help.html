C3D Help

Welcome
C3D is a Token, an Exchange and a Platform, designed to benefit holders, from everyday use and growth. By holding C3D Tokens you automatically earn Dividends, which are paid for by the fees collected on purchase, sale and transfer of the token. These tokens can and will be used in games, Dapps, development and store of value applications.

Dividends and Fees
On every Transfer, Trade, Buy or Sell of C3D tokens, there is a 10% fee charged to the transactor - This fee is distributed among all other holders of C3D tokens, as "Dividends". As more people and businesses use the Smart Contract and ecosystem, these fees will begin to make up somewhat of a "Universal Basic Income".

2. Buy C3D Tokens

  1. Navigate to the C3D Dashboard, and enter an amount of ETC to spend on C3D Tokens. When you enter a number, an estimate of how many tokens you'll receive will appear below the entry field. Please note, this is without fees taken into consideration.
  2. Click the "Buy" button in the window, and approve the transaction from your Wallet.

3. Compound your Dividends

  1. 1. Navigate to the C3D Dashboard, and look at your Dividends (next to "My Wallet" on a computer, beneath it on mobile).
  2. 2. Look at the Buy Price of 1 C3D Token. If you have more Dividends than it costs to buy a token, this is a good time to Compound.
  3. 3. Tap "Reinvest", and confirm the Transaction in your wallet.

4. Sell C3D Tokens

  1. Navigate to the C3D Dashboard, and enter an amount of C3D tokens to sell for ETC. When you enter a number, an estimate of how much ETC you'll receive will appear below the entry field. Please note, this is without fees taken into consideration.
  2. Click the "Sell" button in the window, and approve the transaction from your Wallet.

5. Withdraw ETC

    • If you have Dividends, you can tap "Withdraw", and they will be transferred to your wallet.
    • If you do not have Dividends, you might need to sell some tokens. Follow the Sell instructions, then "Withdraw".
