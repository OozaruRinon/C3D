Introduction


Welcome
C3D is a Token, an Exchange and a Platform, designed to benefit holders only, from everyday use and growth. By holding C3D Tokens you automatically earn Dividends, which are paid for by the fees collected on purchase, sale and transfer of the token.

Dividends and Fees
On every purchase, sale and transfer of C3D tokens, there is a 10% fee charged to the transactor - This fee is distributed among all other holders of C3D tokens, as "Dividends". As more people and businesses use the Smart Contract and ecosystem, these fees will begin to make up somewhat of a "Universal Basic Income".
